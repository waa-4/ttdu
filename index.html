    How to Play

    The cube walks automatically. Use WASD, arrow keys, or the mobile pad for small movement adjustments. Click or tap the game to shoot.

    The boulder appears after 30 seconds, starts far away, and follows your vertical movement so you cannot escape it by moving lanes.

    Kill zombies, grab crates, buy guns, finish challenges, and rebirth for permanent speed.
