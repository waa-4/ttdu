    How to Play

    Your cube moves automatically. Zombies chase you, and shooting them gives bonus money.

    The boulder catches you after your safe-distance meter runs out. Upgrade rock distance to make each run longer.

    Rebirth after reaching the required distance. Each rebirth permanently adds 5% speed and increases the next distance requirement.

    Worlds unlock automatically as your best distance increases.
